Word-Meaning GAME

This game is for educational purposes and is very beneficial for students from Class 1 to 12, as well as for people of any age group.

Game Rules

  • Play this game with your friend or anyone who knows English words and their meanings
  • One player fills one cell with any alphabet
  • Then the other player fills another cell to form a meaningful word
  • For example, if your friend fills 'M', then you fill 'E', and if you know the meaning of "ME" (मुझे), your points increase by 2
  • Words must be completed from left to right or top to bottom. For example, 'CAR' is correct but 'RAC' (right to left) is not
  • Points are awarded based on word length - a 3-letter word earns 3 points
