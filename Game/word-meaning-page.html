How to Play the Word Meaning Game

  • Play this fun and educational game with a friend, family member, or anyone who wants to improve their English vocabulary and word skills.
  • Players take turns filling one cell with a single alphabet letter to build words.
  • The goal is to form meaningful English words by placing letters either from left to right or top to bottom.
  • For example, if your friend fills the cell with 'M', you can add 'E' next to it to form the word "ME". Knowing the meaning of "ME" (‡§Æ‡•Å‡§ù‡•á) will earn you 2 points.
  • Words must be spelled correctly from left to right or top to bottom ‚Äî words spelled backward like "RAC" instead of "CAR" do not count.
  • Points are awarded based on the length of the word formed; for instance, a 3-letter word earns 3 points, encouraging you to build longer words.
  • This game helps improve vocabulary, spelling, and meaning recognition, making learning fun and interactive.
